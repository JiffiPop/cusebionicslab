Motivation

My name is Jeffrey Alan Houston.  I graduated from Syracuse University in 2020 with a B.S. in Aerospace Engineering.  I developed this website to serve as a portfolio to display my work.

When I was young, my favorite pastime was going to the Air Zoo Aerospace & Science Museum in Portage, MI.  I then progressed to designing aircraft and spacecraft for missions in Kerbal Space Program in my free time and went on to take an advanced physics course during high school.

These encounters inspired me to study Aerospace Engineering to further my understanding and interest in different disciplines of engineering.  Based on my experience with mechanical design, I am looking to obtain a job where I can apply these skills to enhance a business's products.
